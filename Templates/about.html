About Us

Rented is a simple and convenient platform that lets you rent out the items you own or find items you need to borrow. All you have to do is post your item, set a price, and make it available to others. From tools and electronics to furniture and more, Rented makes it easy to share, save, and earn without the hassle of buying new.
